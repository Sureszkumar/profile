About

The person behind the code

I grew up in Chennai, studied Computer Applications at SSN College of Engineering, and spent the early part of my career at Cognizant — building enterprise systems and learning what it means to deliver software that holds up under real-world pressure.

After nearly eight years, I moved to Rabobank ICT in Utrecht, where I led teams building payment systems for one of Europe's largest banks. Living and working in the Netherlands shaped how I think about collaboration — the Dutch directness, the flat hierarchies, the deep respect for quality craftsmanship.

"Good software isn't just code that works. It's code that the next person can understand, trust, and build on."

At Thoughtworks in London, I found the environment where I could do my most meaningful work — leading from discovery through to production, owning the technical vision, and helping teams grow. The payment platform I built for a major holiday business is the work I'm proudest of.

I'm a genuine advocate for AI-first engineering — not as a trend to follow, but because I've seen firsthand how thoughtfully embedding AI into architecture reviews and code workflows raises the bar for everyone on the team.
